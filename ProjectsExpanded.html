E-learning Web App project
Overview
Young and Capable is a e-learning entrepreneurial web application. This platform was purposed for a school web development project. It is still being modified.
Challenge
A classmate and I share a passion in solving the issue of unemployment and fostering job creation in Africa. Our scope of focus was South Africa as it is where we are both from. South Africa has a very high unemployment rate with many college students graduating only to not find jobs because of a lack of experience.
Approach
This platform’s aim is to equip students with the necessary skills to enter the work force or create their own businesses. The platform has features such as learning material, an opportunity board to access shared opportunities, a discussion forum to exchange knowledge among peers and a self assessment quiz. The self assessment quiz is meant to assess a student’s current skill set, passions, strengths, weakness and relevant information that can help us cater opportunities to them. The project is still ongoing.
