School Food Ordering Mobile App
Overview
GRUB is a food ordering mobile application concept formed to make the food court experience, at the African Leadership University easier. It enables a more efficient process reviewing of orders, preparing the food and delivering it to the student without hassel.
Challenge
After a long day in class, students face long queues, a delay in their orders or not receiving what they have ordered. There is little preparation and organization time for the vendors at the food court when handling a large quantity of students.
Approach
This mobile app would allow students to make orders in advance and share any feedback that could help the tech team or the vendors improve on the services provided. Vendors are able to track orders thus delivering on time and meeting customer satisfaction.
