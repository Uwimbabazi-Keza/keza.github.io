Email Design Template
Overview
This an email design template for a small future jewellry business, that my friends and I plan on launching soon.
Challenge
We simply wanted to experiment with different themes and colors for different marketing aspects of our business. The email template is an example of this.
Approach
Most color schemes and designs we have noticed in our competitors are pinks and neutral colors. Our business is centered around regality and elegance so I thought to opt for a regal emerald theme here.
